# Brand Spec — Dr. Benjebara Ghali Dental Clinic

## Color Palette (OKLch & Hex)
- **Primary Teal:** `#00B5B0` | `oklch(72% 0.16 195)` (Accent)
- **Dark Teal:** `#007A78` | `oklch(52% 0.12 195)` (Emphasis/Border)
- **Page BG:** `#F5F8F8` | `oklch(97% 0.005 195)` (--bg)
- **Text:** `#1A1A1A` | `oklch(20% 0.01 195)` (--fg)
- **Accent Blue:** `#0099CC` | `oklch(65% 0.15 230)` (Secondary accent)

## Typography
- **Display:** "Poppins", system-ui, sans-serif
- **Body:** "Montserrat", system-ui, sans-serif
- **Mono:** "JetBrains Mono", monospace (for numerics)

## Layout Posture
- **Radius:** 16px (radius-sm), 24px (radius-md)
- **Border Weight:** 1px soft luminous borders (Glassmorphism style)
- **Accent Budget:** Used for primary CTAs and eyebrows.
- **Glass Effect:** 74% opacity white surfaces with 12px blur.
